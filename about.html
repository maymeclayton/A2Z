About Us

My name is Michael Mitchell. I am from Frankfort where I have resided most of my life. I was raised by hardworking parents and that spirit was instilled in me at a young age. I have worked in customer service for years and customer satisfaction has always been my main goal. Prior to doing this work professionally, I was a DIY-er and learned how to do many things.

Since 2014 I have been doing this work for other people. I truly enjoy the work and love the challenges that come from the varying jobs that I have done. I take great pride in my work and I will treat your home as if it were my own. As a family man, I know first-hand how important a properly functioning home really is. As such, I will always strive to do the job to your satisfaction. After all we want a customer for life, not just a day!
